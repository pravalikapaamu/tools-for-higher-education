About Us

Tools for Higher Education is a study abroad platform developed as a minor project using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The website provides information about international universities, academic programs, admission guidance and student support.

Our Mission

To help students choose the right university and course according to their interests and career goals.

Our Vision

To make higher education information simple,accessible and useful for students planning to study abroad.
